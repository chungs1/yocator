For Charity
Malaika is a four year-old from Haiti who suffers from atrioventricular canal defect, a condition that prevents blood from flowing properly in the heart. Her condition requires treatment, a costly operation of $1500.

You can help her! If YOTHEWORLD gains the most subscribers and Yo's by July 4th, YOTHEWORLD will donate Yo Hackathon's most subscribed award of $2500 to her cause. Any spare money will be donated all other children on Watsi.

What's YOTHEWORLD?
Yo is simple, and that's why it's gotten a lot of buzz. Watsi is also simple, but hasn't received the attention it deserves. We're showing just how simple it can be to help the lives of others.
